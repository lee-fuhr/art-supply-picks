Quick answer

The Art 101 Budding Artist's Doodle & Draw Art Set is our top budget pick for all-around creativity and ease of use. The Prina 50-Piece set is ideal for kids who favor drawing and sketching, while the Soucolor 104-piece set suits families or classrooms that want maximum variety.

A well‑chosen art supply set can turn a bored afternoon into a burst of creativity, but the sheer number of options, and the risk of buying cheap, frustrating materials, makes the decision tricky. The best sets balance quantity with quality: enough pieces to spark exploration without overwhelming a beginner, and durable enough to survive eager hands. When shopping on a budget, focus on three things: variety of media (crayons, colored pencils, markers, pastels), a sturdy case for organization, and age‑appropriate tools (washable for younger kids, finer tips for older ones). Avoid sets that cram in dozens of identical items or flimsy accessories, you want tools that actually work well from the start.
